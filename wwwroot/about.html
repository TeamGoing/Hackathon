Just like you, the team behind Explore Vermont has a passion for the outdoors, especially for the slopes and wintery paradises of Vermont. We are so pleased to provide this wonderful service to everyone to share the enjoyment that skiing and snowboarding can bring into someone's life.

This web application was developed as part of the HackVT competition in 2013. Our team consisted of Robert Bethune, Matt Dean, Jake Ellenberg, and Samantha Ovitt. Our team studied at Champlain College and went to the competition as a student group.
